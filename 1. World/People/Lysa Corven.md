<font color="#7f7f7f">Lysa Corven is a small-name fixer in **[[Chendl]]**—the kind of person who makes the city function in the gaps between **knightly honor** and **noble politics**. Officially she’s nobody: a job handler who passes along “simple errands” for the **[[Knightly Conclave]]** and, when pressure or coin demands it, the occasional deniable request from **[[Noble Council]]** intermediaries. Unofficially, she’s a living switchboard of contacts: couriers, tavern-keepers, watch sergeants, clerks, and junior knights who all owe her _one little favor_.</font>

## Description
### Appearance

- **Build**
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">- **Height:** slightly below average (about 5'3" / 160cm)</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Frame:** lean and wiry—more **runner’s endurance** than brawler strength</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Posture:** always angled like she’s halfway to leaving; weight on the balls of her feet</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Hands:** quick, steady, ink-stained fingertips; faint calluses from rope, wax, and courier satchels</font>
- **Features**
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Face:** sharp cheekbones, a quick half-smile that never fully relaxes</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Eyes:** grey-green, constantly tracking exits, pockets, and mood shifts</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Hair:** dark brown, worn up in a practical twist with a pin (sometimes a small bone or bronze pin)</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Tell:** taps two fingers against her thumb when thinking—like counting risks</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Marks:** a thin, old scar at the edge of her jaw (knife-close, not heroic), and faint freckles across her nose</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Scent:** faint beeswax and paper (seals and notes), with rain-damp wool</font>
- **Clothes**
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Outerwear:** a weathered **ashen-grey cloak** (her “signature”), hem patched, clasp plain but sturdy</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Layers:** fitted wool tunic in muted tones, reinforced at elbows; a sleeveless vest with inner pockets</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Belt & gear:** narrow belt with a coin purse, wax-seal kit, chalk nub, small notebook, and a **ring of three keys** (not all to doors—some to boxes, desks, and trust)</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Boots:** soft-soled riding boots for quiet movement; always well-oiled</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Jewelry:** minimal—usually just a simple cord with a small saint’s token tucked under her shirt</font>
	- <font color="#7f7f7f">**Overall impression:** she dresses to **blend**, move, and carry secrets—never to impress, but always to look like she belongs wherever she stands.</font>
