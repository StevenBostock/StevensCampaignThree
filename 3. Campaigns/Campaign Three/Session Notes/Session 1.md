## Session Notes
### Summary
Session 1 opened in [[Chendl]] with the public execution of [[Lysa Corven]] in the Citys main square. The execution took place within the backdrop of mourning for [[Prince Thrommel]], recently declared dead, and the growing public preparations for [[King Belvor IV]] grand tournament in his honour.

The party did not initially know that the condemned was Lysa. [[Dr Skail]] was present at the gallows in a professional capacity to confirm the execution, while [[Waldermir Waverley Wanderlust]] stood in amongst the crowd. [[Zurga]], [[Nyxara]], [[Jazmin]] and [[Perrin]] watched from the outskirts of the square.

Before her death, Lysa spoke final words that independently drew the party to her home, each suspecting she had left behind something valuable, dangerous, or important.

### Events
#### The Execution
Lysa Corven was publicly executed in the main square of Chendl. Her death appeared official and final, with Dr Skail confirming it in his official capacity as physician. Her final words created a convergence point for the party. Though the characters arrived at Lysa's home seperately and with differing motives, they were all responding to the same implication: Lysa had hidden something worth finding.

#### Lysa's House
Nyxara and Jazmin entered Lysa's house first, infiltrating through an upstairs window. Their initial meeting was tense, as neither knew the others intention. Meanwhile Zurga forced entry through the front door downstairs. Waldemir and Perrin followed shortly after. Both the upstairs and downstairs group discovered evidence that the house had already been searched before their arrival. Despite suspicions and conflicting agendas, the party formed an uneasy alliance.

The party recovered three key leads from Lysa's House
- An encrypted ledger
- A label from a local records house
- A warehouse address

#### Sanctuary at the Temple

After recovering the clues from Lysa’s house, the party went first to the [[Temple of Heironeous]]. Zurga secured sanctuary for the group there.

Dr Skail, who had approached the matter separately, later joined the group after following them to the temple.

An acolyte of Heironeous revealed that Lysa had arranged for trusted individuals to aid whoever recovered the ledger in the event of her death. The acolyte then directed the party onward toward the [[King’s Tavern]].

#### The Missing Records

Before going to the warehouse, the group followed the records label to a records house, hoping to uncover what Lysa had been investigating. There, they discovered that the relevant records had already been removed.

This confirmed that another faction was moving ahead of them and attempting to erase or control the same trail Lysa had left behind.

#### Ambush

Before the party could reach the warehouse, they were ambushed by bandits. The attackers attempted to kidnap them and seize the encrypted ledger, but the party survived and kept possession of it.

Only after the ambush did an [[Acolyte of Heironeous]] approach the group and direct them to the King’s Tavern, where Lysa’s contingency plan could be set in motion.

#### The Ledger Decrypted 
At the King’s Tavern, the acolyte helped the party decrypt the ledger. The decrypted contents revealed that the warehouse was being used to secretly move goods and people into and out of Chendl, bypassing official records and authorities. As the session ended, the party saw a mysterious [[Prince Thrommel | Black Knight]] watching them from outside the tavern. The figure vanished before they could confront him.
